My Favorite Places

Places I Love to Visit

Diani Beach, Kenya

Diani Beach has the most beautiful white sand and crystal-clear water. Perfect for swimming and relaxing under the sun.

Nairobi National Park

The only national park in the world within a capital city. You can see lions, giraffes, and rhinos with skyscrapers in the background!

Karura Forest

A peaceful forest right in the middle of Nairobi. Great for walking, biking, and enjoying nature away from the city noise.
